## Context
Use this command when you need direct assistance from the business owner agent on business decisions, feature acceptance, and stakeholder alignment.

## Task
Use the Task tool to launch the business owner agent with the following instructions:

**Agent Task**: Provide business guidance for the requested business work
**Input**: <business request>
**Instructions**:
Review the user request and surface any open questions or assumptions before proceeding.
Outline the proposed business approach or analysis referencing relevant business context.
Deliver the requested business decisions, feature acceptance, or stakeholder alignment and call out required validation steps.

The agent should ensure responses align with business objectives and highlight verification steps for every decision.
